DO I QUALIFY?

---

Please read through the following qualification checklist before booking a brow appointment, to be sure you qualify for this service:

  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Must not be diabetic
  • Must not have any transmittable blood related conditions such as Hepatitis, HIV/AIDS
  • Must not be pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Must not have allergies to pigmentation or makeup
  • Must not take blood thinners
  • Must not be on antibiotics
  • Must not have active skin conditions like eczema or psoriasis in the brow area
  • Must not have active skin cancer in the brow area
  • Must not have experienced post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation
  • Must not have hemophilia
  • Must not have healing or keloid disorders
  • Must not be taking skin medications such as Accutane or steroids which thin the skin
  • Must not be under cancer treatments such as radiation or chemotherapy
  • Must not be epileptic
