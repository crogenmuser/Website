Microblading

A semi-permanent cosmetic tattoo procedure that creates hairlike strokes by placing pigment under the skin. Great for thinning eyebrows, or just to create more density.

Powder Brows

A form of tattooing to fill in thinning areas of the hair, or to conceal a hair transplantation scar.
