WEST CHESTER, PENNSYLVANIA

"Microblading" - a semi-permanent cosmetic tattoo procedure that creates hairlike strokes by placing pigment under the skin. Great for thinning eyebrows, or just to create more density.

"Scalp Micropigmentation" - a form of tattooing to fill in thinning areas of the hair, or to conceal a hair transplantation scar.
